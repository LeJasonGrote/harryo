Our Team

Harry "O"

DJ, Video DJ, MC, Choreographer

Harry started his career in the entertainment field at a very early age as a dancer. While still in High School, Harry was awarded a full scholarship to train at The Joffrey Ballet School summer intensive program in New York.

After graduating Harry continued his training as a paid apprentice for the prestigious contempory dance company "Hubbart Street Dance Chicago"and when on to join the Actors Equity Association & dance in several musicals for Sacramento Music Circus, Las Vegas Production Shows, Norwegian & Princess cruises, & most recently the hit Irish show "Spirit Of The Dance". Today, Harry's greatest passion is providing the best experience for your event.

Joey D

Music Director, DJ/Video DJ

Las Vegas’ DJ Joey D developed a passion for music at age 15, after purchasing his first set of turntables and started playing music for neighborhood parties. By age 20, he began spinning for some of the top venues in the tri-state area. Including the legendary Palladium and the world famous Tavern on the Green in New York City.

At age 27, Joey opened Joey D’s Records, catering to DJs and music professionals.His expertise and knowledge about music attracted DJs from New York, New Jersey and Connecticut, for music and advice on programming.

In 1998, Joey accepted the position of Head DJ at Lily's Beach Club in the Horizon Casino Resort, Lake Tahoe, Nevada. By 2002, Joey had become Head DJ and Music Director at Studio 54 in the MGM Grand, and has since held residencies and spun events at; Venus, Tangerine, Lucky Strike, Pure,Cherry, Tryst, XS and many others. Today, Joey has become one of Las Vegas' premiere music authorities, still spinning at the top venues in Las Vegas. His skills both as a DJ and music expert keep him at the forefront of industry.

Jazz Dimazana

DJ, MC, Sound Engineer

Working in the music and entertainment industry since 1985 as a musician, sound and lighting engineer, and stage technician, Jazz has been the Lead Sound Engineer for MGM Grand Adventures Theaters, Smokin Hot Aces nightclub at The Venetian, and Blue Martini nightclub in Las Vegas. He worked behind the scenes with numerous production shows in Las Vegas, including EFX with David Cassidy, EFX Alive with Rick Springfield, Michael Flatley's Lord Of The Dance, Jay Leno Live at the MGM Grand, Showgirls Of Magic, and many others.

Alan Hott

DJ, MC

Alan is a native of Peoria, IL and moved to Las Vegas in 2007. Alan has DJ'd for several events under Harry "O" Productions. Alan is completely dedicated to DJing and MCing and will ensure your event flows smoothly and is stress-free.



Kevin Leon

Entertainment Coordinator, Choreographer

Kevin specializes in jazz, lyrical, contemporary, and musical theatre. His credits include “Masquerade Show In the Sky” at the Rio Hotel and Casino, principal dancer in several cruise ships and has had the opportunity to perform with such stars as Usher, Enrique Iglesias, Mary Tyler Moore and MC Hammer.

Kevin has also been featured in many industrials, such as the grand opening of the Wynn Hotel Casino, the Detroit International Auto Show, Redken and Beauty Craft Hair Shows, a Rite Aid commercial, the Windsor Casino and many more.

Along with his performing, Kevin’s choreography has been awarded many regional and national titles. You may even spot some of his students in The Lion King on Broadway, in the Radio City Rockettes, Cruise Ships, and the national tour of Cats. Currently, Kevin is the Company Director of Innovation Dance Company in Las Vegas, Nevada. With his motivation and coaching, Kevin has led the competitive team to many overall awards.

Samantha Hunt

Entertainment Coordinator, Choreographer

Originally from Chesterfield, Michigan, Samantha Hunt specializes in modern, lyrical, jazz, stretch and improv. With over 25 years of dance training, she has found herself fully involved with every aspect of the dance world. Her training includes not only in class experience, but one on one mentoring with Dee Caspary, Dennis Caspary, Gus Giordano (The Jazz Dance World Congress), Ray Savage, Mia Michaels, Jason Parsons, and Lauren Adams. Whether it be performing, teaching, directing, or judging, Samantha loves it all.

Loving to judge, Samantha has been judging for dance competitions for over ten years now. She hopes to make an impact on the up and coming dance stars around the country with her advice and love for the art of dance.

Throughout her performance career, Samantha has worked in numerous shows in Las Vegas. Her credits include: “Masquerade Show in the Sky”, Buck Wild, The Sopranos, Bite, and Sin City Comedy. She has also performed in many industrials, including: Beauty Craft and Maly’s Hair Shows, the NFL Thanksgiving Day Half Time Show and the Detroit Shock Team. Samantha has also worked alongside many celebrities throughout her performing years, such as Debbie Allen, Paula Abdul, and Enrique Iglesias, to name a few.

Currently, you can spot Samantha in Las Vegas, proudly serving as the Assistant Director for Innovation Dance Company. As a teacher, she is well known for her creative concepts and visualizations in her choreography. Samantha has a knack for getting young dancers to open up in class and really express themselves through dance. She has been sought after for her choreography all over the United States, and enjoys traveling to share her love of dance with artists around the country. Her choreography, creativeness and hard work have won numerous awards and many outstanding ovations. In 2011, her choreography was highlighted by the FDC and contributed to Innovation Dance Company earning the right to be called the number one dance company in the Northwest Region of the United States.
